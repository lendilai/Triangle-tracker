Types of triangles

Equilateral triangles

These have all sides being equal.

Isosceles triangles

These have only two of their sides being equal.

Scalene triangles

For these, none of the sides is the same.

Right-angled-triangles

These are scalene triangles with one angle being equal to 90 degrees.

Area of a triangle

For right-angled triangles, the formula is
(0.5 * base * height)

Heron's formula is used for any triangle.
